This light-weight hybrid app was created at an all-night hack-a-thon. Its main purpose is to help find your car after a long day at the mall in a crowded parking lot. Using Toyota's API, and Google-Maps API to send and receive information back and forth to give crisp and direct turn by turn navigation from your phone to your vehicle leaving you without worries of either, approaching the wrong vehicle ( which can turn out to be very dangerous ), or just walking a mile and a half to the other side of the mall parking-lot because men don't need to get directions from anyone ( we never learn) Hope you find it useful.
